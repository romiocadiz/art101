Psceudocode for a Salsa

This is our Pseudocoding for Making Salsa

Gather Ingredients

  1. Tomatoes
  2. Jalape単os
  3. Cilantro
  4. salt
  5. pepper

Make sure you have cooking supplies

  1. Stove
  2. Cast Iron Skillet
  3. Blender
  4. Bowl
  5. Tongs

Make the Salsa

  1. Wash and dry hands and then vegetables
  2. Place cast iron skillet onto stove
  3. Turn on fire to high
  4. place tomatoes and jalape単os onto the hot skillet
  5. Flip occasionally until all ides of the tomatoes and jalape単os are black and blistered
  6. Turn off stove
  7. Use tongs to pick up the tomatoes and jalape単os
  8. Place in the blender and blend until semi-smooth
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste
  10. Blend to combine
  11. Roughly tear up clilantro with hands
  12. Add to blender
  13. Blend for a couple seconds
  14. Pour into bowl
  15. Enjoy!
